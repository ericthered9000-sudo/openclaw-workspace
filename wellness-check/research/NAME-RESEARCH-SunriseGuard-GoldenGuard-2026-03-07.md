## Detailed Analysis

### SunriseGuard - Risk Assessment

**Strengths:**
- No direct app conflict
- No direct USPTO trademark
- Unique compound name
- Positive connotations (sunrise = new day, hope)

**Risks:**
- Sunrise Senior Living is a major brand in same demographic (seniors)
- Could face trademark opposition if they claim consumer confusion
- Would need to verify "Sunrise" isn't protected in Class 9/42

**Recommendation:**
1. File trademark search at USPTO for "SunriseGuard" in Class 9 and 42
2. Consult trademark attorney about Sunrise Senior Living risk
3. If cleared, register domain immediately
4. Proceed with trademark filing

**Overall Risk Level:** LOW to MEDIUM

---

### GoldenGuard - Risk Assessment

**Why GoldenGuard is NOT available:**

1. **Direct App Conflict:** Golden Guard VPN exists on Google Play
2. **Registered Trademark:** GOLDEN GUARD is trademarked for supplements
3. **Multiple Companies:** Insurance, security, VPN companies using the name
4. **Domain Taken:** Multiple golden-guard domains already owned

**Overall Risk Level:** HIGH - Do not proceed
