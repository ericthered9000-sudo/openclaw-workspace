# App Name Research - March 7, 2026

## Executive Summary

Your current app name "Wellness Check" has a **direct conflict** with "Circle Alert Wellness Check In" by Carlton Media, LLC (launched 2021) — a competitor app with nearly identical features. Additionally, "wellness check" is a descriptive/generic term that's difficult to trademark.

**Recommendation:** Rebrand to a distinctive, protectable name before launch.

## Trademark Filing Cost Estimate

- **TEAS Plus:** $250/class (if you use pre-approved descriptions)
- **TEAS Standard:** $350/class (custom descriptions)
- Classes needed for app: **Class 9** (software), possibly **Class 42** (SaaS)
- Total estimate: $250-$700

### Recommended Classes

- **Class 9:** Computer software and mobile apps
- **Class 42:** Software as a service (SaaS) platforms

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Tier Structure

| Plan | Price | Features | Target User |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic check-in, 911 button, 1 family contact | Trial / Low-income |
| **Premium** | $6.99/mo or $59/yr | Full features, wellness score, weekly reports, unlimited family | Solo senior |

1. **Annual discount incentivizes commitment** — 28% retention vs 12% for monthly
2. **Family plan captures caregivers** — They're often the ones paying
3. **Free tier drives discovery** — Essential in health/safety category

5. **Positioned between competitors** — Below Snug Plus ($99/yr), above Circle Alert ($40/yr)

## Monetization: Free Tier Ad Strategy

### Ad Banner Implementation

**Option 1: Top Banner (Recommended)**
- Small horizontal banner at top of screen
- Doesn't block main content
- Can be dismissed after 5 seconds on free tier

**Option 2: Bottom Banner**
- Fixed position at bottom
- Less intrusive but may conflict with navigation

**Option 3: Interstitial Ads**
- Full-screen ads between screens
- More revenue, but worse UX
- Limit to 1 per session for free users

### Ad Revenue Estimates

| Metric | Conservative | Optimistic |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| Free users | 4,750 (95%) | 9,500 (95%) |
| Impressions/user/day | 5 | 10 |
| CPM (Cost per 1,000 views) | $1.50 | $3.00 |
| Monthly ad revenue | $1,070 | $2,850 |

**Note:** Health/senior apps typically earn $1-3 CPM. Interstitial ads can earn $5-15 CPM but hurt UX.

### Recommended Ad Networks

| Network | CPM Range | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Google AdMob | $0.50-2.00 | Banner ads, easy integration |
| Meta Audience Network | $1.00-3.00 | Higher CPMs, more targeting |
| Unity Ads | $3.00-8.00 | Interstitial/video (if needed) |

### Best Practice: Non-Intrusive Ads

For senior apps, **minimal disruption is critical**:

1. **Top banner only** — doesn't interfere with main UI
2. **No ads on emergency screens** — 911 button, SOS should never have ads
3. **No video ads** — seniors may struggle to close them
4. **Subtle design** — match app color scheme, not flashy
5. **Easy to ignore** — small font, minimal movement

### Ad-Free as Upgrade Incentive

| Feature | Free | Premium ($59/yr) |
|---------|------|------------------|
| Ad-free experience | ❌ | ✅ |
| This alone can drive 10-15% of free users to paid | | |

Many apps report **15-20% of upgrades cite "remove ads" as the primary reason**.
