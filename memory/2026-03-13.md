# 2026-03-13 - Tarot Decision MVP Built

## Major Pivot Decision
James proposed a **new business idea**: Tarot card reading app as a standalone product (not tied to HomeBeacon). 

### Market Research Done
- Analyzed top tarot apps: Labyrinthos, Trusted Tarot, Mystic Mondays, Golden Thread, Co-Star
- Key insight: Market saturated with **subscription-based** apps
- **Gap identified**: No one-time purchase model + decision-focused AI readings
- Opportunity: Decision-making tarot (career, relationships, life path) vs. generic daily readings

### MVP Decision
- **One-time purchase** ($6.99) instead of subscription
- **Decision angle**: "Should I take this job?" not just "what's my energy"
- **3-card spread**: Situation → Choice → Outcome
- **AI-powered interpretations**: Contextual to user's actual question

## What We Built Today
### Tarot Decision Demo - Live MVP
- **Tech stack**: Vanilla HTML/JS, tarotapi.dev (free RWS card API)
- **Deployed**: https://tarot-decision-demo.vercel.app
- **Features**:
  - Decision category picker (Career, Relationship, Life Path, Money, Health, Custom)
  - 3-card draw from real API
  - Position-based meanings (Situation/Choice/Outcome)
  - Reading generated from card meanings (MVP without AI for now)
