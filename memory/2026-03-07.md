## FINAL NAME DECISION: HomeBeacon (March 7, 2026)

### Why HomeBeacon Won
- No competing apps on Play Store or App Store
- No USPTO trademark in Class 9 (software) or Class 42 (SaaS)
- Existing "HomeBeacon" companies are in real estate (different industry)
- Perfect meaning: "beacon in the home" for senior safety
- Easy to spell, easy to remember
- Positive connotations (home, beacon, safety, guidance)

### Names Rejected (Conflicts Found)
- Wellness Check → Circle Alert (direct competitor)
- GuardianWell → Guardian Life Insurance trademark
- GoldenGuard → Golden Guard VPN app exists
- SunriseGuard → Sunrise Senior Living (major brand in same demographic)
- WellBeacon → Castlight Health + BCBS Alabama app
- HealthBeacon → HealthBeacon Limited (medication adherence company)
- MyHomeBeacon → MyHomeBeacon LLC (emergency beacon product)
- SafeBeacon → SafeBeacon app (safety tracker)
- CareBeacon → CareBeacon (caregiving/fall detection)

### Available Alternatives (Not Chosen)
