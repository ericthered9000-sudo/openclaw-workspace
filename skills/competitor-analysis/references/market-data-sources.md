# Market Data Sources

## Free Sources

### App Store Data
- **App Annie (Sensor Tower)** - Free tier available
- **Appfigures** - Rank tracking, some free data
- **Play Store Console** - Category rankings
- **App Store Connect** - Category rankings (Apple)

### Market Size Data
- **Statista** - Some free reports, paid for detailed
- **IBISWorld** - Executive summaries free
- **Grand View Research** - Press releases with key numbers
- **MarketWatch** - Market size articles
- **Business Insider** - Industry analysis articles

### Funding & Company Data
- **Crunchbase** - Free tier available
- **PitchBook** - Limited free data
- **AngelList** - Startup information
- **LinkedIn** - Company sizes, employee counts

### Reviews & Sentiment
- **App Store reviews** - Direct user feedback
- **Google Play reviews** - Direct user feedback
- **TrustPilot** - B2C reviews
- **G2, Capterra** - B2B reviews
- **Reddit** - Community discussions

## Paid Sources

### Comprehensive Market Data
- **IBISWorld** - $1,000+ per report
- **Euromonitor** - Custom pricing
- **Gartner** - Enterprise pricing
- **Forrester** - Enterprise pricing

### App Intelligence
- **Sensor Tower** - $100+/month
- **App Annie (data.ai)** - $100+/month
- **Apptopia** - Custom pricing
- **Mobile Action** - Custom pricing

## Search Queries for Market Data

### Market Size
```
"[category] app market size 2024"
"[category] app market size 2025"
"[category] software market report"
"[category] industry size billion"
```

### Growth Rates
```
"[category] app CAGR"
"[category] market growth rate"
"[category] industry trends 2024"
```

### Funding & Competition
```
"[category] app funding rounds"
"[category] startup acquisitions 2024"
"[competitor] funding history"
```

## What to Look For

### Market Size Indicators
- Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)

### Competitive Landscape
- Number of competitors
- Market concentration (top 3 share)
- Recent exits/acquisitions
- New entrants

### User Demand Indicators
- Search volume trends (Google Trends)
- App store search rankings
- Reddit/community discussions
- Feature request forums

## Citation Format

When citing market data:

```markdown
**Market Size:** $X billion (2024)
**Source:** [Organization], [Report Name], [Year]
**URL:** [Link if available]
```

Example:
```markdown
**Health App Market:** $1.76 billion (2025)
**Source:** Grand View Research, Personal Safety App Market Report, 2024
**CAGR:** 13.7%
```
